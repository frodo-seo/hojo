Hojo is an open-source, serverless personal finance app.

Snap one screenshot and Hojo logs your expenses, income, and asset holdings — or let it auto-parse payment notifications from your card and banking apps. All transactions and settings are stored only in your device's IndexedDB. No cloud sync, no accounts, no central server. Not even the maintainers can access your ledger.

AI features (classification, parsing, monthly and yearly reports) run on a BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) model — you provide your own Anthropic and Datalab API keys. No dependency on the publisher's infrastructure or credits.

<b>Features</b>
• Screenshot-first input — receipts, payment notifications, payslips, bills, brokerage, exchange screens
• Notification auto-parsing — Haiku reads card/pay/bank notifications and extracts amount, merchant, and category
• 5-way auto classification — expense, income, recurring expense, recurring income, asset trade
• Asset portfolio — stocks/ETFs, crypto, commodities (gold/silver/platinum) with live prices
• Net worth aggregation with FX conversion + pie chart
• Monthly budgets with usage tracking
• Monthly and yearly AI reports
• Korean / English UI + AI report language sync
• 9 PM logging reminder
• CSV export

<b>Required API keys</b>
• Anthropic — classification, parsing, reports (console.anthropic.com)
• Datalab — OCR (datalab.to)

You pay your own API usage. Typical personal use is very low cost.
