Hojo is an open-source, serverless personal finance app.

All transactions and settings are stored only in your device's IndexedDB. No cloud sync, no accounts, no central server. Not even the maintainers can access your ledger.

AI features (receipt OCR, monthly and yearly reports) run on a BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) model — you provide your own Anthropic and Datalab API keys. No dependency on the publisher's infrastructure or credits.

<b>Features</b>
• Record expenses and income with categories, memos, and dates
• Manage recurring income/expenses
• Monthly budgets with usage tracking
• Receipt camera OCR with automatic parsing
• Monthly and yearly AI reports
• 9 PM logging reminder
• CSV export

<b>Required API keys</b>
• Anthropic — for AI reports and receipt parsing (console.anthropic.com)
• Datalab — for receipt OCR (datalab.to)

You pay your own API usage. Typical personal use is very low cost.
